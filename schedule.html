Daily Study Rhythm

Weekday Session (1–1.5 hrs)

  • 0:00–0:05 — Open today's guide. Scan the objectives.
  • 0:05–0:55 — Read the narration. No skimming. Highlight what surprises you.
  • 0:55–1:15 — Do the flashcards for today's section (if available).
  • 1:15–1:30 — Self-check questions at the end of each session.

Weekend Session (1 hr)

  • Saturday: Review the week's content. Re-read anything fuzzy. Flashcard review.
  • Sunday: Practice quiz for the domain just finished. Target 80%+. If below — note the weak topics, re-read those sections Monday.
Pace Rule

Don't move to the next domain until you score 80%+ on the current domain's quiz. That's the gate. If you score below 80%, you get one targeted re-read day before re-quizzing. The exam is 150 questions in 4 hours — 1.6 minutes per question. Speed comes from depth, not shortcuts.
