Exam Registration & Scheduling

Register early. Lock the date. The commitment makes the prep real.

Booking the exam date is the single most important study decision you can make. It creates a real deadline. Pick the date first, then work backwards.

Suggested lead times: CISM — book 6 weeks out. CISSP — book 8 weeks out. AWS / Azure / GCP — book 4–6 weeks out, these are more tactical, less cramming-intensive.
